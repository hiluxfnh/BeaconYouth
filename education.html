    Education

    We expand learning through digital & civic literacy, mentorships, leadership camps, and deep school partnerships — prioritising girls and underserved students.

    🎓 Digital Literacy 🤝 Mentorship 🏕️ Leadership Camps 🏫 School Partnerships

    💻

    Digital Literacy

    Hands-on modules covering internet basics, cyber safety, productivity tools, and fact-checking — building confident, critical digital citizens.

    • Internet basics & safe browsing
    • Productivity (docs, spreadsheets, slides)
    • Fact-checking & media literacy
    • Intro to AI & digital rights
    🌟

    Leadership & Mentorship

    Peer mentors, alumni, and professionals guiding career choices, civic engagement, and life skills — particularly for girls and first-generation learners.

    • Peer-to-peer mentorship circles
    • Professional role model sessions
    • Civic engagement & leadership skills
    • Annual Youth Leadership Camp
    🎓

    Scholarships & Support

    As funding allows, we channel small bursaries, equipment support, and school supply assistance — with transparent, merit-and-need criteria.

    • Small bursaries for at-risk students
    • Equipment & device support
    • Transparent public selection criteria
    • Priority for girls & underserved youth
    🏫

    School Partnerships

    We co-design clubs, bootcamps, and inclusive classroom activities with teachers and parents. All resources are open and reusable — any school can adopt them for free.

    • Joint after-school digital clubs
    • Teacher capacity-building workshops
    • Parent engagement sessions
    • Open curriculum shared under Creative Commons
