What We've Done

Work we're proud of

From school pilots to global forums — here's a glimpse of our impact so far.

🚀
BYC Official Launch — 2025
Milestone

Beacon Youth Collective Goes Live

After months of co-design with young people across Cameroon, BYC formally launched in 2025 with adopted statutes, an executive committee, and pilot programs underway in schools across Bertoua.

🌍
School Climate Clubs Pilot
Climate

Youth-Led Climate Clubs in Schools

Piloting climate literacy clubs that blend hands-on adaptation projects with community mapping using the EcoCityAdapt approach.

🔒
Digital Rights & Cyber Safety
Tech

Cyber Safety Workshops for Youth

Interactive sessions teaching digital rights, online safety, and responsible AI use — reaching over 150 young people in community centres.

🌐
Global Representation
Advocacy

Youth Voice at CSW & COP

BYC's founder represented Cameroonian youth at international forums including the UN Commission on the Status of Women and COP climate negotiations.
