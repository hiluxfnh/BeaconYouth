    Gender Equality & Ending GBV

    We prevent and respond to violence against women and girls through awareness, allyship with men & boys, SRHR education, and survivor-centered referral pathways.

    Prevention & Allyship

    Evidence-based curricula for schools and communities — engaging boys and men as active allies, addressing harmful norms respectfully but firmly.

    • School-based GBV prevention modules
    • Boys & men allyship workshops
    • Community norm-change campaigns
    • Trained peer facilitators
    ❤️

    SRHR Education

    Evidence-based adolescent sexual & reproductive health and rights sessions — adapted from humanitarian toolkits and delivered safely by trained facilitators.

    • Age-appropriate SRHR content
    • Consent & healthy relationships
    • Menstrual health & hygiene
    • Safe referrals to health services
    📍

    Referral Mapping

    Clear, survivor-centered pathways to trusted local services — with strict confidentiality, do-no-harm principles, and psychosocial support integration.

    • Local service mapping & directories
    • Confidential reporting channels
    • Psychosocial first aid training
    • Case management coordination
    🔒

    Safeguarding & PSEAH Commitment

    Safeguarding, anti-harassment, and PSEAH (Protection from Sexual Exploitation, Abuse and Harassment) policies apply to all BYC staff, volunteers, and partners. We hold ourselves to the highest standards.

    All facilitators are trained in do-no-harm principles and survivor-centred approaches before engaging with communities.
