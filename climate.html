    Climate Action

    We mainstream climate literacy, support community adaptation & nature-based solutions, and amplify youth voices in local and global policy spaces.

    🏫 School Climate Clubs 💧 Community Adaptation 🌐 UNFCCC / YOUNGO 🗺️ Risk Mapping
    📚 Education 🌿 Climate Action 💻 Tech for Good 🤝 Gender & GBV
    🏫

    School Climate Clubs

    Interactive, youth-led clubs tackling waste, water, trees, and local adaptation — guided by peer mentors using practical, hands-on approaches.

    • Waste reduction & sorting projects
    • Tree-planting & green space creation
    • Water conservation demonstrations
    • Youth-made climate risk maps
    💧

    Community Adaptation

    Practical demonstrations on rainwater harvesting, soil restoration, heat-risk awareness, and nature-based solutions for vulnerable groups.

    • Rainwater harvesting systems
    • Soil restoration & composting
    • Heat-risk awareness campaigns
    • Nature-based urban cooling
    🌐

    Policy Engagement

    Youth briefs, consultations, and participation in UNFCCC/YOUNGO processes — turning community data into real policy recommendations.

    • Youth climate policy briefs
    • Representation at COP & YOUNGO
    • Community data for decision-makers
    • Stakeholder consultation sessions
    🗺️

    EcoCityAdapt Integration

    We adapt the EcoCityAdapt platform into school-friendly modules — covering climate basics, risk mapping, and practical solutions tailored to urban and peri-urban Cameroonian schools.

    • School-ready climate literacy modules
    • Teacher guides & facilitation tools
    • Community risk mapping activities
    • Student-led adaptation project templates
