Beacon Youth Collective

    Empowering youth through education, climate action, technology, and equality

    Beacon Youth Collective is a youth-driven nonprofit based in Bertoua, Cameroon. We help young people build practical skills, lead local action, and shape safer, more equitable communities.

    Our Pillars

    What we focus on

    We work across four connected areas so young people can lead change with skills, support, and community partnerships behind them.

    Education

    Digital and civic literacy, mentorship, school partnerships, and learning opportunities that expand what young people can access.

    Explore Education

    Climate Action

    Climate clubs, adaptation projects, community mapping, and youth participation in practical local climate solutions.

    Explore Climate

    Tech for Good

    Cyber safety, digital rights, AI literacy, and youth-led problem solving that turns technology into public good.

    Explore Tech

    Gender & GBV

    Prevention education, safer communities, survivor-centered referrals, and allyship that strengthens dignity and protection.
