Beacon Youth Collective
    About

    Who we are

    Beacon Youth Collective is a youth-led nonprofit based in Cameroon, advancing education, climate action, technology for good, and gender equality through practical community-rooted programs.

    Mission

    Empower young people to lead change through skills, opportunity, collaboration, and safe spaces that strengthen their communities.

    Vision and values

    We believe young people should shape the solutions that affect them. BYC centers integrity, inclusion, participation, climate and social justice, and community accountability.

    Approach

    We co-design with schools, local groups, and partners, combining youth voice, evidence, and practical action on the ground.

    Governance

    BYC is guided by executive leadership, program leads, and a growing advisory network that keeps the organization accountable and responsive.

    Where we work

    We are headquartered in Bertoua and collaborate across Cameroon through schools, community spaces, partnerships, and online networks.
