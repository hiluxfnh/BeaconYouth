    About BYC

    Who we are

    BYC is a youth-led nonprofit founded by young people in Cameroon, advancing education, climate action, technology for good, and gender equality — together.

    🎯

    Our Mission

    Empower young people to lead change — through skills, opportunities, and safe spaces — so that communities become more educated, resilient, and equitable.

    🌟

    Our Vision

    A world where all young people thrive and have meaningful agency in shaping the solutions that affect their lives, communities, and futures.

    🤝

    Our Approach

    We co-design with schools, councils, and local NGOs — blending programs, policy linkages, and partnerships backed by data and authentic youth voice.

    🏛️

    Governance

    Simple and accountable: Founder/ED, Treasurer, Secretary, Program Leads across Education, Climate, Tech & GBV, and a Youth Advisory Council.

    📍

    Where We Work

    Headquartered in Bertoua with activities across Cameroon and partnerships across Africa — in schools, community centres, and online.

    Our Core Values

    ✅ Integrity 🤝 Inclusion 💬 Participation 🌱 Climate Justice 💡 Collaboration 🛡️ Do-No-Harm
    Our Story

    How BYC came to be

    2024

    The idea takes shape

    A group of young people in Bertoua begins co-designing what a youth-led, evidence-driven collective could look like — grounded in local realities, connected to global movements.

    2025

    BYC officially launches

    Statutes adopted, executive committee formed, and first school pilots kick off — marking the formal birth of Beacon Youth Collective in Bertoua, Cameroon.

    Now

    Growing impact

    250+ youth reached, 10+ partner schools, representation at the UN CSW and COP — and much more ahead as we scale our four core programs across the country.

    🌐 English governs all official communications. A French version is in development and will be published as capacity allows.
