Programs

Education

We expand learning through digital & civic literacy, mentorships, leadership camps, and school partnerships—prioritizing girls and underserved students.

Digital Literacy

Hands-on modules: internet basics, cyber safety, productivity, and fact-checking to build confident digital citizens.

Leadership & Mentorship

Peer mentors & role models—alumni and professionals—guiding career choices, civic engagement, and life skills.

Scholarships

As funding allows, we channel small bursaries and equipment support, with transparent criteria.

School Partnerships

We co-design with teachers and parents: clubs, bootcamps, and inclusive classrooms. We share open resources that any school can reuse.
