    Privacy Policy

    This Privacy Policy explains how Beacon Youth Collective (BYC) collects, uses, and safeguards your information across our website, programs, events, and online services.

    1. Who we are

    Beacon Youth Collective (BYC) is a youth-led nonprofit based in Bertoua, Cameroon. We work across Education, Climate Action, Technology for Good, and Gender & GBV. Contact us at beaconyouthcollective@gmail.com.

    2. What data we collect

    • Contact data: name, email, phone number, organization, role.
    • Program data: responses to forms, applications, surveys, event registrations.
    • Technical data: IP address, device/browser info, approximate location, pages visited (analytics).
    • Media: photos, audio, or video from events (with consent, where applicable).

    3. How we collect data

    • Directly from you via forms (contact, get involved, donations), emails, or messages.
    • Event sign-ups, attendance lists, and consent forms.
    • Cookies and analytics tools for basic usage metrics.
    • Partners that refer you to our programs (with appropriate consent/agreements).

    4. Why we use your data

    • To respond to inquiries and provide services you request.
    • To manage program participation, events, mentorships, and training.
    • To send updates you opt into (e.g., newsletters), which you can unsubscribe from anytime.
    • To improve our website and understand impact (anonymized analytics).

    5. Legal bases for processing

    • Consent: you’ve given clear consent for specific purposes.
    • Contract: processing needed to deliver a service you requested.
    • Legitimate interests: running and improving programs in ways that do not override your rights.
    • Legal obligation: compliance with applicable laws and reporting duties.

    6. Sharing and disclosure

    • Service providers: platforms for email, analytics, hosting, and form processing.
    • Program partners: schools, NGOs, and councils, only for relevant/consented activities.
    • Legal requests: when required by law or to protect safety, rights, or property.

    7. Data retention

    We keep personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes above, then delete or anonymize it. Retention varies by program, legal requirements, and donor/partner obligations.

    8. Your rights

    • Access, rectify, or delete your data where applicable.
    • Object or restrict processing in certain situations.
    • Withdraw consent at any time for activities based on consent.
    • Complain to a supervisory authority if you believe your rights are violated.

    To exercise rights, email privacy@beaconyouth.org.

    9. International transfers

    BYC may process data outside your country. When we do, we use appropriate safeguards (such as contractual clauses) consistent with applicable law.

    10. Children & safeguarding

    We prioritize youth safety. We do not knowingly collect data from children without appropriate consent and safeguarding measures. Consent and participation protocols are adapted per program and age group.

    11. Cookies & analytics

    We use minimal, privacy-friendly analytics to understand traffic patterns. You can control cookies via your browser settings. If we adopt additional tracking, we’ll update this policy and provide choices.

    12. Security

    We implement administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect data, including access controls, encryption in transit, and staff awareness. No method is 100% secure, but we strive for best practices.

    13. How to contact us

    Email: privacy@beaconyouth.org or beaconyouthcollective@gmail.com. Postal address available upon request.

    Updates: We may revise this policy periodically. When we do, we’ll update the “Last updated” date below and, where appropriate, notify you.
