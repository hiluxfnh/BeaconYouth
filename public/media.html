    About BYC — standard copy

    Use this approved text in articles, press releases, and event descriptions. Please do not modify it.

    Beacon Youth Collective (BYC) is a youth-led, evidence-driven nonprofit organization based in Bertoua, Cameroon. Founded in 2025, BYC empowers young people across the country through four core programs: Education, Climate Action, Tech for Good, and Gender Equality & GBV Prevention. BYC co-designs its programs with schools, local councils, and partner organizations to ensure community relevance and sustained impact. The organization has reached over 250 young people and is active in more than 10 partner schools. BYC has been represented at international forums including the United Nations Commission on the Status of Women (CSW) and COP climate negotiations. For more information, visit beaconyouthcollective.org or contact beaconyouthcollective@gmail.com.
