About

Who we are

BYC is a youth-led nonprofit founded by Young people, based in Cameroon, advancing education, climate action, technology for good, and gender equality.

Mission

Empower young people to lead change—through skills, opportunities, and safe spaces—so that communities become more educated, resilient, and equitable.

Vision & Values

We envision a world where youth thrive and shape solutions. Our values: integrity, inclusion, participation, climate & social justice, collaboration, and do-no-harm.

Approach

We co-design with schools, councils, and local NGOs. We blend programs, policy linkages, and partnerships—supported by data and youth voice.

Governance

Simple and accountable: Founder/ED, Treasurer, Secretary, Program Leads (Education, Climate, Tech, GBV) and a Youth Advisory Council.

Where we work

Headquartered in Bertoua with activities across Cameroon and partnerships across Africa. We work in schools, community centers, and online.
